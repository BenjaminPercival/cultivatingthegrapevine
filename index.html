Research Interests

I'm in finl year of a PhD at Liverpool Univerity under the supervision of Alon Faraggi in the String and BSM Phenomenology Group. My research interests are:

  • Exploring phenomenological characteristics of classes of models in the string landscape. In particular, those arising from Z_2 x Z_2 orbifold compactifications.
  • Issues of tachyonic instability and the cosmological constant problem for non-supersymmetric strings.
  • Interpolations of string partition functions in different dimensions.
  • Applying sophisticated computational techniques to classification problems in string theory, such as machine learning and, recently, Satisfiability Modulo Theories (SMTs).
